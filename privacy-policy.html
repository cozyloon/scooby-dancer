Privacy Policy — Scooby Dancer Browser Extension

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Overview

Scooby Dancer is a cosmetic browser extension that displays an animated dancer character on web pages. This extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data or browsing information of any kind.

Data Collection

We collect no data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing history or URLs are tracked
  • No cookies are set or read
  • No network requests are made by the extension
  • No analytics or tracking of any kind is used
  • All dancer animations and assets are bundled locally within the extension

Local Settings

The extension saves a small set of preferences using your browser's local storage: the dancer you selected, whether the dancer is shown or hidden, and the dancer's position on the page. This information is stored only on your own device so your choices persist across pages and browser sessions. It is never sent to the developer or any third party, and it is removed if you uninstall the extension.

Permissions

The extension injects a visual overlay on web pages solely to display the Scooby Dancer animation. No page content is read, modified, collected, or transmitted.
